Who am I ?

I am currently a student in Terminale S with the SVT specialty, always passionate about graphics, music and cinema. In the perspective of contributing to these arts, I have developed some skills, as a self-taught. I was thus able to learn drawing, piano and guitar.
I am encouraged and surrounded by my family and friends. I have developed a certain sociability which leads me to particularly appreciate working in a group.

Where I come from

A Toulouse native marked by the end of the world

I was born in Toulouse on April 1, 2002. I have lived in the centre of Toulouse since my birth. A parenthesis nevertheless marked my childhood, with a one-year stay in Sydney, Australia, when I was 3 years old. I grew up around sports, like gym, music and middle school, then high school. I am currently preparing a BAC in Science.

WHY DO I LIKE MUSIC ?

Listening and playing music is for me a joy of every moment. My sensitivity allows me to perceive the emotion carried by the author on his piece. I try to reproduce this emotion with my instruments, without preventing me from improvising. Whether it's the piano or more recently the guitar, I've learnt on my own, by listening to or watching how others play.
